Who We Are

We are Nakshatra Astronomy Club, an enthusiastic group of engineering students united by a shared passion for astronomy.

Our club serves as a hub for curious minds, blending engineering principles with the exploration of the cosmos. Through collaborative learning, projects, and events, we strive to deepen our understanding of the universe while innovating and discovering new possibility.

What We Do

Workshops

We organize workshops and seminars that provide hands-on learning experiences and deep dives into various astronomical concepts, tools, and technologies.

Stargazing Nights

Our club hosts regular observation sessions where members use telescopes to explore celestial objects, from the Moon to distant galaxies.

Research Projects

We encourage members to participate in research projects that apply engineering skills to solve challenges in astronomy, such as building telescopes, developing software, or analyzing astronomical data.

Collaborations

We collaborate with other clubs and participate in national and international competitions, fostering teamwork and showcasing our skills in astronomy and engineering.

Guest Lectures

We invite astronomers, engineers, and industry experts to share their insights and experiences, providing members with exposure to the latest advancements in the field.

Outreach Programs

Our club engages with the community through outreach programs, including school visits and public talks, to spark interest in astronomy and inspire the next generation of engineers and astronomers.
