# Nakshatra Astronomy Club - Official Website

Welcome to the **Nakshatra Astronomy Club** website, where we aim to empower, encourage, and educate enthusiasts about the fascinating world of astronomy and its connection with engineering.

## Features

- **About Us**: Learn more about our club's mission, vision, and goals in promoting astronomy.
- **Events Section**: Explore past and upcoming events like telescope-making workshops, science visits, and fun activities.
- **Gallery**: View images from our Moon Photography Contest and other memorable moments.
- **Contact Us**: Get in touch with us for collaboration or membership inquiries.

## Tech Stack

- **HTML5/CSS3**: For structure and styling.
- **JavaScript**: Interactive elements.
- **GSAP**: Animations for modern and engaging UI.
- **Swiper.js**: For carousels and galleries.
